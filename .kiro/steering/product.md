# Product Overview

HyperCore Gift Draw Platform — a single-use web app for a fundraising raffle.

## What it does
- Team members register ticket buyers (name + phone) via an admin panel
- Generates downloadable digital tickets (PDF, Google Wallet, Apple Wallet)
- Runs a randomized draw selecting 3 winners from active tickets
- Publishes winning folio numbers on a public results page
- Shows a fundraising dashboard with progress toward the goal

## Who it's for
- Team HyperCore (5 members, Universidad Tecmilenio) — organizers
- Family, friends, and general public — ticket buyers

## Why it exists
Fundraising for the team's trip to the KIA Mexico Innovation MeetUp 2026 finals in Cancún.
Goal: $26,000 MXN from the raffle (200 tickets × $200 MXN).
